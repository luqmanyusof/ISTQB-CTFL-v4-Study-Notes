# ISTQB CTFL v4.0 – Group Activity
## Chapter 2: Functional & Non-Functional Testing

---

### The Difference

| | Functional | Non-functional |
|---|---|---|
| **Question** | Does it work? | Does it work *well*? |
| **Tests** | What the app *does* | How the app *performs* |
| **Examples** | Feature behaves correctly | Speed, security, usability, reliability |

---

### App Assignments

### Steps

**Step 1 — Pick your app & list 3 main features** *(2 min)*
> e.g. Pizza app: place order, track delivery, payment

**Step 2 — Write 6 functional + 6 non-functional test cases** *(10 min)*
> One sentence each — what are you testing and does it pass or fail?

**Step 3 — Present to the class**
> Read each test case, say "Functional" or "Non-functional – [type]". Other groups vote 👍 or 👎.

---

### Example — Pizza Delivery App

**Functional**
1. User can place an order with 2 pizzas and the total price is calculated correctly.
2. User receives an order confirmation after successful payment.
3. User can cancel an order within 5 minutes of placing it.

**Non-functional**
1. The order page loads in under 3 seconds. *(Performance)*
2. Payment details are encrypted and not visible in the URL. *(Security)*
3. A first-time user can place an order without any help. *(Usability)*
