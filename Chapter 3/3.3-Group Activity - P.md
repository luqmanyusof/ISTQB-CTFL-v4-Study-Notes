# ISTQB CTFL v4.0 – Group Activity
## Chapter 3: Static Testing

### What is Static Testing?

| | Static Testing | Dynamic Testing |
|---|---|---|
| **How** | Review a document without running anything | Execute the code / system to test |
| **When** | Early — before execution | After the system / code is ready |
| **Example** | Review a requirement, test case, or recipe | Run the app, click the button |

> **Today:** You will review a food recipe. The goal is not to cook it — but to find problems in the document. That is static testing.

### Steps

**Step 1 — Read your recipe** *(3 min)*
> Read the full recipe first before starting your review.

**Step 2 — Find the problems** *(10 min)*
> Use the review sheet below. Look for anything that feels wrong — missing ingredients, unclear instructions, missing steps, or contradicting information.

**Step 3 — Suggest improvements** *(5 min)*
> For every problem you find, write down how you would fix it.

**Step 4 — Present to the class** *(3 min per group)*
> Share the problems you found and your suggestions. Other groups can add on if they spot something you missed.

### Demo — 🧋 Milo Ais

Ingredients:
- Milo
- Hot water
- Condensed milk
- Ice

Steps:
1. Add Milo into a glass.
2. Pour in hot water and stir.
3. Add condensed milk.
4. Serve cold.

### Recipe — Group A 🥪 Sardine Sandwich

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 1 can of sardines in tomato sauce
- Onion
- Red chilli
- Salt
- Butter

Steps:
1. Heat the sardines in a pan and mash lightly.
2. Add salt and stir well.
3. Spread butter on the bread.
4. Place the sardine filling on one slice of bread and close with the other slice.
5. Serve.

---

### Recipe — Group B 🍳 Onion Omelette

Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- Salt
- Cooking oil
- Black pepper powder

Steps:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat well.
2. Slice the onion thinly.
3. Heat the pan over the stove.
4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until firm.
5. Serve hot.

---

### Recipe — Group C 🍊 Orange Juice

Ingredients:
- 3 oranges
- Water
- Sugar
- Ice

Steps:
1. Cut the oranges in half and squeeze the juice into a blender.
2. Add water and blend until smooth.
3. Strain the juice into a glass.
4. Add ice and serve.

---

### Recipe — Group D 🍌 Pisang Goreng

Ingredients:
- 4 bananas (pisang tanduk)
- Wheat flour
- Salt
- Oil for frying

Steps:
1. Peel the bananas and slice lengthwise.
2. Mix flour with water until thick.
3. Dip the bananas into the batter.
4. Fry until golden brown.
5. Remove and serve.
